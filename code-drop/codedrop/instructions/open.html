Your goal

The blue cube is the generator. It drops blue marbles.
The green cube is the goal.
The weird object with three prongs is your hand. You use your hand to create objects that will direct the path of the marbles.
Your goal is to get 3 blue marbles to hit the goal. You will manipulate the path of the marbles by writing a program that adds objects to the scene. To enter the programming environment, press ` (below esc). You can press it again to leave the programming environment.
