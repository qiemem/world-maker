Numbers

While you can string multiple .forward(1.0)s together to move objects different distances, this doesn't give you very fine grained control.
Enter hand.forward(1.0).
Mouse over the 1.0. Notice how it highlights a little and a circle appears on the left side of it.
You can click and drag this circle to increase or decrease the number! Try it now and see what happens to the hand. This lets you quickly try out a whole range of numbers to get exactly what you want.
